Socials

We’re pleased to announce our long-awaited LeedsMUN 2016 social pack!

LeedsMUN is renowned for its parties! However, there are only a limited amount of tickets on sale, so it is vital for you get a place quickly. You can purchase a social pack on our LUU Webpage.

From there, you can click on the ‘Shop’ tab, and select the ‘Leeds MUN Conference Social Pack 2016’ to add to basket. Please note that if you are not a Leeds student, you will either need to create a guest account, or log in to the account you used to purchase the conference ticket.

The Brotherhood

We’ll be starting our Friday night in our very own Old Bar for some food and drink before heading in to town for an amazing night at The Brotherhood of Pursuits and Pastimes.

The Brotherhood exists to entertain the gentlefolk of Leeds with the finest sporting events and classic drinks, including expertly concocted cocktails and craft beers of distinction. Not to mention artisan pies to die for.

The Hilton Leeds

Our Saturday night social takes place in the vibrant heart of central Leeds and where better to relax and get to know your fellow delegates better than the Hilton Hotel.

Indulge in a sophisticated three-course meal followed by music and dancing until late. With plenty of wine and soft drinks provided, and a DJ for the party afterwards we’re sure it’s going to be a social to remember.
