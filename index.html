Partners

We are proud to announce our partnership with Cardiff University International Model United Nations Conference 2016. This year their conference is under the theme of ‘The Post 2016 Outlook of the United Nations’, and will take place between the 19th and 21st of February.

Cardiff prides itself on being a cheap and affordable option compared to other university conferences. Their delegate fees are at a reasonable £20 for an individual, and £17 for a group of three or more. To further help with the costs, they are offering a limited selection of accommodation packs, at a bargain of £80 for two nights. The application deadline for the accommodation offer is the 20th of January, with the payment deadline being the 5th of February – as it is first come first served, all interested delegates should send their applications ASAP!

See their website for more details on the event, and how to apply.
