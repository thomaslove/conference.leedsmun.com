Logistics

Getting Here

By Train

Leeds station connects us with all major UK cities. For train information see the National Rail Enquiries website. 

By Air

Leeds is served by two major airports: Leeds Bradford and ­Manchester Regular flights are available from a huge number of European and International cities. Leeds Bradford airport is approximately 7 miles away from the University, so easily commutable by taxi.

Bus and Taxi

University of Leeds is approx 10 min bus ride away from the Train Station and will only cost you £1. Bus stops are all either immediatly adjacent to the staion or a very short walk from it. See it on the map.

Most widely used route numbers are: 1, 6, 56 and 93. If unsure, just ask the driver wherether the bus goes past the University of Leeds and he/she will be happy to help!

Taxi fares will be depend on whether the car has been pre-booked or not. If you choose to take a readily avaliable cab directly from the Taxi Car Park, across the road from the main station entrance, it will cost you approximately £5, depending on traffic. A taxi booked in advance will cost less and there is an adundance of book-only taxi services in Leeds. Always be careful and ensure you only board a licensed cab!

By car

If you are using sat nav, the address is:

University of Leeds, Woodhouse Lane, Leeds, LS2 9JT. Please note street listing can appear as Cavendish Road in some navigation systems

Leeds is linked to the M1 and M62. Parking on campus is very limited.

Parking for disabled "blue badge" holders is available on campus for visitors. If you are a blue badge holder you are advised to enter the campus via the main barrier on Woodhouse Lane to show your badge. You can then be directed to the nearest available disabled parking bay to the area you are visiting. Blue badge holders do not need to pay to park on campus.

Staying Here

Unfortunately the Secretariat is unable to offer accommodation to delegates. However, Leeds is a big vibrant city that has a great range of places to suit all tastes and pockets. For best avaliability and deals, remember to book early! Take a look at the list of various accomodations we have compiled below - they are located close to the railway station, and all allow easy access the University by a short bus or taxi ride. We also recommend visiting www.booking.com to search for alternate accommodations, if necessary.
