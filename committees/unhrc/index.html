The Topics

The question of freedom of press and censorship in the digital age

This topic will focus on issues of freedom of press in the context of modern medias such as through the internet and social media; it is about defining and promoting the right of individuals and organisations to circulate opinions and communications, while also considering individual country's censorship and security laws which have traditionally inhibited on these liberties.

The question of the death penalty

Article 3 of the UN Universal Declaration of Human Rights says : “Everyone has the right to life, liberty and security of person”. Right to life focuses on the principle of having the right to live and not have your life taken away by another human being, with emphasis being on abortion, capital punishment etc.

The death penalty is one of the most controversial issues in modern times in relation to Right to Life. Many states use death penalty as a means of punishment and deterrence for various crimes. But, we live in a world where many times we cannot come to consensus on which acts should actually be criminalized. While many oppose death penalty for being inhuman and cruel, the supporters would say its needed to tackle heinous crimes such a murders, rape and terrorism. Death Penalty and Right to Life have a very thin line between them.
