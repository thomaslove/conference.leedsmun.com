CRISISOf Machines and MenUSA - PARKINSON B.11 (FRI/SUN) AND 4.20 (SAT)
EU - PARKINSON B.22

The year is 2020, and artificial intelligences have quickly become integral to the running of almost all of the worlds systems in recent years. After a series of electronic attacks on infrastructure targets around the world, no one is any closer to finding out who, or what, is behind these attacks. Join either the US or EU cabinet and prepare to investigate the attacks and deal with the perpetrators.
